Shadow and Bone

Alina Starkov is a young soldier who learns she has a rare power to summon light. In the divided land of Ravka, she trains with the magical Grisha and faces the Darkling, who wants to use her abilities. The book explores identity, courage, and fighting against evil.

A Tale of Two Cities

This story takes place in London and Paris during the French Revolution. It follows Charles Darnay, Sydney Carton, and Lucie Manette. Themes include love, sacrifice, and justice. Carton’s choice to give his life shows how people can change and find meaning even in dark times.

The Aventure of Tom Sawyer

Tom Sawyer is a playful boy living near the Mississippi River. He enjoys tricks, adventures, and exploring with his friend Huck Finn. The story shows childhood freedom, friendship, and learning right from wrong. Tom’s adventures mix humor with lessons about bravery and growing up responsibly.
